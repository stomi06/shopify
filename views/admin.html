How to set up the Free Shipping Bar:

Follow these steps to set up your free shipping bar

1. Configure the Free Shipping Bar appearance in the app settings.

2. Go to your store’s theme editor.

3. Click "Add section" in the left panel.

4. Under "Apps", choose "Free Shipping Bar".

5. Click "Save" in the top-right corner.

6. Open your website to see the bar in action.
